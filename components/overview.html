What is Hole People?

Hole People is a simple yet highly addictive strategic puzzle game where players must fill all the stickmen into the holes before their slots run out. Different colors block each other, so every move requires careful planning and quick thinking.

The objective is straightforward but challenging: empty the front rows to make space, manage your available slots wisely, and ensure every stickman falls into the correct hole. One wrong move can fill your slots too quickly — and it’s game over!

With regular updates, the game continues to evolve. The latest version introduces the CAPE MAN — red-cloaked stickmen who must be timed correctly with their cloaks open to drop into the holes. Alongside smoother visuals, polished animations, and brand-new levels, Hole People keeps puzzle fans engaged and challenged.

Whether you’re playing casually or aiming for three-star victories on every level, this game delivers endless hours of fun, strategy, and satisfying puzzle-solving.
